About Linq

Linq is people. Team members, attendees, partners, supporters, speakers, contractors, volunteers and the list goes on. They build companies, develop products, use latest tech to change the world and meet at Linq to share their journey.

Why we do what we do

We aim to put Cyprus on the tech event map of the world. We want to gather leading minds, visionaries, entrepreneurs, and professionals from all over the world here in Cyprus to further pormote our island as one of the major European IT Hubs.

How we do this

Year to year we bring tech-lovers together to share ideas, collaborate and grow, all while having a great time and exploting Cyprus from perspectives they never knew existed.
